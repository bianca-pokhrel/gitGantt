gitGantt... a new way to plan your week

created by: bianca pokhrel

When working on short term projects, it is often a challenge to plan tasks and measure progress. gitGantt allows you to incorporate Gantt charts with regular day-to-day to-do lists to provide you with the ultimate way of keeping your projects on schedule. With gitGantt you can add, remove, and edit tasks




When you first create a Gantt chart, an empty Gantt chart is created with the top row signifying the days of the week separated by lines. You can see that the Gantt chart is pretty small. Add tasks to it to make it larger!


Let's add some more tasks!

Now that we have some tasks in place, we can delete and also change the names and start/end days of them!


Lastly, we can change our themes. In this version of the library, we have 4 available themes including the default theme. You can choose additional presets such as "summer day", "autumn morning", and "winter night". Here they are in order below!
